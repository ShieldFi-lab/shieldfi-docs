## Problem

Crypto users and DeFi protocols lose billions each year to exploits, compromised wallets, and smart contract vulnerabilities.

---

## Solution

ShieldFi provides automated protection using real-time risk intelligence and parametric smart contracts.

Features:
- Wallet risk scoring
- Parametric insurance policies
- Automated claim payouts
- Protocol risk monitoring

---

## How It Works

1. Risk Intelligence Engine analyzes wallet activity
2. User purchases coverage policy
3. Risk Oracle updates risk scores on-chain
4. Trigger engine executes automated payouts
