# ShieldFi Protocol Risk Engine

ShieldFi calculates a real-time risk score for DeFi protocols.

The score is calculated using on-chain metrics:

- TVL volatility
- Oracle price deviation
- Liquidity imbalance
- Whale withdrawal patterns
- Governance activity

Each metric contributes to the final risk score.

Risk scores influence:

- insurance premium pricing
- coverage limits
- payout risk management

This system allows ShieldFi to dynamically adapt coverage to changing market conditions.
