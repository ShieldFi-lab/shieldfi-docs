ShieldFi Revenue Model
ShieldFi generates revenue from multiple sources across the decentralized protection ecosystem.
The protocol is designed to serve retail users, DeFi protocols, and institutional partners.

1️⃣ Insurance Premiums (Primary Revenue)

Users pay premiums to protect their assets against exploits, hacks, or depegs.

Example:

User deposits $10,000 in DeFi
User buys ShieldFi coverage
Premium = 2% per year
User pays $200 premium

Revenue flow:

Premium Paid
     │
     ▼
Coverage Pool
     │
     ├─ 70% → Liquidity Providers (yield)
     ├─ 20% → ShieldFi Treasury
     └─ 10% → Risk engine / protocol maintenance


2️⃣ Risk Scoring API (Infrastructure Revenue)
ShieldFi’s risk engine can be exposed as an API.

External platforms could query:

Protocol risk score
Wallet risk score
Smart contract exploit probability
Liquidity health score


Possible users:
• DeFi dashboards
• exchanges
• crypto lenders
• institutional funds

Example API pricing model:

Basic API → free
Professional → $2,000/month
Institutional → $10,000+/month

3️⃣ Institutional Integration

Large institutions entering crypto need risk protection infrastructure.

ShieldFi could offer:

Protocol coverage
Smart contract protection
Liquidity insurance
Custody wallet protection

Example:

Crypto fund deposits $50M in DeFi
Buys ShieldFi institutional coverage
Premium = 1.2%
Annual revenue = $600,000


4️⃣ Bank + Crypto Wallet Integration

Retail banks beginning to support crypto wallets may require risk layers.

Possible integration:

Retail bank
     │
     ▼
Customer crypto wallet
     │
     ▼
ShieldFi risk layer

Example:
Customer takes a loan against crypto collateral.
ShieldFi can provide:

Wallet exploit protection
Collateral depeg protection
Smart contract risk monitoring

Revenue model:

Bank pays ShieldFi protection fee
OR
Customer pays protection premium

5️⃣ Liquidity Pool Yield Fees

ShieldFi liquidity providers supply capital for coverage pools.

Example:

LP deposits $1M into coverage pool
Premiums collected from users
LP earns yield from premiums

Protocol can take:
10–15% performance fee

6️⃣ Parametric Event Fees

ShieldFi triggers automatic payouts for measurable events.

Examples:

Stablecoin depeg
Protocol hack
Liquidity collapse
Oracle manipulation

Protocol integrates ShieldFi risk triggers
Pays integration fee
Pays monthly monitoring fee
