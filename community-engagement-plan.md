# ShieldFi Community Engagement Plan

ShieldFi aims to build a focused early community around DeFi security and protection infrastructure.

The goal is not rapid growth but meaningful engagement with developers, researchers, and DeFi users.

## Early Community Channels

Primary channel:

Telegram Group

Purpose:

- discussion of DeFi security
- research collaboration
- early contributor participation
- project updates

## Early Contributors

Target group size: 10–30 early contributors.

Ideal participants:

- DeFi developers
- smart contract auditors
- DeFi traders
- security researchers

## Contributor Roles

Early contributors may help with:

- reviewing protocol architecture
- suggesting parametric trigger conditions
- testing smart contract deployments
- identifying potential exploit scenarios
- providing feedback on risk scoring models

## Community Updates

ShieldFi will provide consistent updates including:

- development progress
- research findings
- security discussions
- new architecture proposals

## Research Discussions

The community will periodically analyze major DeFi exploits to understand how protection systems can be improved.

Topics may include:

- exploit patterns
- oracle manipulation
- flash loan attacks
- liquidity drain events

## Future Incentives

Early contributors may be recognized through future community rewards such as:

- early contributor NFTs
- governance participation
- ecosystem recognition
