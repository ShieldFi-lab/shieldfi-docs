# Thread: DeFi Needs Protection Infrastructure

Tweet 1:
DeFi unlocked a new financial system.

Permissionless.
Composable.
Global.

But there is one layer still missing.

Protection infrastructure. 🧵

Tweet 2:
Over the past few years, billions of dollars have been lost to smart contract exploits, oracle failures, and liquidity attacks.

Innovation in DeFi has moved faster than the systems designed to protect it.

Tweet 3:
Traditional finance evolved risk infrastructure over decades:

insurance systems  
risk monitoring  
automated safeguards  

DeFi is only beginning to explore these layers.

Tweet 4:
Today, most protection in DeFi is reactive.

Funds are lost.
Investigations begin.
Users hope recovery is possible.

But protection systems should exist *before* failure happens.

Tweet 5:
What if protection could be embedded directly into the protocol layer?

Smart contracts monitoring risk conditions.
Automated triggers responding to events.

Not manual claims.

Automated protection.

Tweet 6:
One model being explored is parametric protection.

Instead of filing claims, predefined on-chain conditions trigger payouts automatically.

If event X occurs → protection activates.

Tweet 7:
This approach could introduce a new infrastructure layer for DeFi:

risk monitoring  
parametric coverage  
automated protection triggers

Systems designed for a decentralized economy.

Tweet 8:
The DeFi ecosystem has built incredible financial primitives.

The next phase may be building the protection infrastructure that supports them.

This is one of the ideas behind ShieldFi.
