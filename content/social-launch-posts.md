Post 2

Why does DeFi keep losing billions to exploits?

Because security and protection infrastructure hasn't evolved as fast as the protocols themselves.

Traditional finance developed risk infrastructure over decades:

• insurance systems
• monitoring frameworks
• automated safeguards

DeFi is only starting to explore these layers.

Protection needs to become part of the protocol stack.
